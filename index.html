Frequently Asked Questions

How is PranBite ghee different from commercial brands? ▼
PranBite is made using traditional Vedic methods with premium quality cow milk. We use slow cooking techniques that preserve nutrients, unlike commercial brands that use high-temperature processing. No preservatives, no additives - just pure ghee.
Is PranBite ghee suitable for all age groups? ▼
Yes! PranBite ghee is 100% natural and suitable for babies, children, adults, and seniors. Rich in vitamins and healthy fats, it's a perfect addition to any diet. For infants, consult your pediatrician before introduction.
How long does the ghee stay fresh? ▼
PranBite ghee stays fresh for 6-8 months when stored in a cool, dry place in an airtight container. Pure ghee doesn't require refrigeration due to its natural preservation properties. Keep away from direct sunlight and moisture.
How quickly will I receive my order? ▼
We deliver within 24-48 hours of order confirmation. All orders are carefully packed in eco-friendly, sealed packaging to ensure maximum freshness and purity when it reaches your doorstep.
Is there a money-back guarantee? ▼
Absolutely! We're confident in the quality of our ghee. If you're not satisfied, we offer a full refund within 7 days of purchase, no questions asked. Your satisfaction is our priority.
Do you offer bulk orders for restaurants/businesses? ▼
Yes! We offer special bulk pricing for restaurants, sweet shops, and businesses. Contact us directly for bulk orders and wholesale rates. We can customize packaging as per your requirements.
